# Interests

Copy to `interests.md` (gitignored). The auto-topic generator (`earworm autogen`)
reads this free-form file to propose new episode topics that match your taste,
steering away from topics you've already covered. Write it however you like —
bullets, prose, a list of beats. Examples:

- The economics and second-order effects of AI on labor markets
- Distributed systems, edge compute, and the tradeoffs of serverless
- How open protocols (RSS, ActivityPub, email) survive platform consolidation
- Long-now / civilizational-scale questions: collapse, resilience, institutions

Be specific about angles you find interesting, not just topics. "What incentive
structures make X happen" produces better briefings than "X".
